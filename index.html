About Us

Established in 1985, Siri Sonuththara Maha Viharaya serves as a center for Theravada Buddhist practice in Boralesgamuwa. Our mission is to promote mindfulness, compassion, and wisdom in modern life.

Our Monastic Leadership

Chief Monk

With over 30 years of service, Ven. Dhammaloka Thero guides the temple with wisdom and compassion.

Ven. Sumedha Thero
